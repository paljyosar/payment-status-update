BILLING & PAYMENT PATTERN:

Bharti Airtel Limited:

Bharti Airtel Limited raises bills on 6th of every month for a period from 5th of last month to 4th of current month. Eg. Bills for the period from 05-Nov-2024 to 04-Dec-2024 is generated on 06-Dec-2024.

To avoid any late fine, the bill is to be paid before next month's bill gets generated.

However, their service continues without disruption till two months' bills become due. When the cycle enters the 3rd month, service discontinues.

Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited:

Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited raises bills on a calendar month basis. Eg. Bills for the month of November-2024 get generated on 01-Dec-2024.

To avoid any late fine, the bill is to be paid in the same month in which the bill is generated.

However, their service continues without disruption for two months without any payment.
