BILLING & PAYMENT PATTERN:

Bharti Airtel Limited:

Bharti Airtel Limited raises bills on the 6th of every month for a period from the 5th of last month to the 4th of the current month. For example, bills for the period from 05-Nov-2024 to 04-Dec-2024 are generated on 06-Dec-2024. To avoid any late fine, the bill must be paid before next month's bill is generated. Their service continues without disruption until two months' bills become due. When the cycle enters the 3rd month, service discontinues.

Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited:

Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited raises bills on a calendar month basis. For example, bills for the month of November-2024 are generated on 01-Dec-2024. To avoid any late fine, the bill must be paid in the same month it is generated. Their service continues without disruption for two months without any payment.
